Questions?

We Have Answers

Is BeAware free to use?

Yes, all core features of BeAware are free. We believe everyone deserves access to product transparency. We may offer a premium subscription in the future for advanced analytics and features.

What kind of products can I scan?

You can scan almost any FMCG product with a barcode, including packaged foods, beverages, shampoos, soaps, lotions, makeup, and household cleaning supplies. Our database is always growing.

Can I use the app for specific allergies or lifestyles?

Absolutely! You can customize your profile for various allergies (nuts, gluten, soy etc.), dietary needs (vegan, keto), and lifestyle values (eco-friendly, cruelty-free) to get personalized alerts.
