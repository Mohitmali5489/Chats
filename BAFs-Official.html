Here's a quick guide to some of our features.

Understanding User Ticks

Ticks are used throughout the app to identify different types of users and channels at a glance.

Gold Tick
Represents an Admin User with special privileges.
Silver Tick
Represents a Special User, like a class representative.
Blue Tick
Represents a Verified User whose identity is confirmed.
Green Tick
Represents an Official Channel for subjects or announcements.
Black Tick
Indicates a confession was posted Anonymously.

About Channels

The "Channels" section on the home page is a dedicated space to deliver important study materials and app-related updates in an engaging chat format. This includes channels for specific subjects, official announcements, and even anonymous surveys to help us improve the app based on your feedback.
